  ```python
  def _assess_llm_confidence(sector):

      complete_count = sum(1 for v in [sector_index, change_pct, 
                                       leading_stocks, fund_flow] 
                           if v not in (None, "", []))
      return "中" if complete_count >= 3 else "低"
  ```
